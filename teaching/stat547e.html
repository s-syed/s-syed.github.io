This course studies modern methods for sampling from complex, multi-modal distributions at scale. Topics include parallel tempering, annealed importance sampling, non-reversible MCMC, sequential Monte Carlo, and their connections to generative modelling. Emphasis is placed on both theoretical foundations and practical implementations in Julia.

</div>

  <div class="project-num">02</div>
  <div class="project-name">Optimal Annealing Paths</div>
  <div class="project-desc">Variational and optimal transport perspectives on path design</div>
</div>

<div class="project-card">
  <div class="project-num">03</div>
  <div class="project-name">Diffusion Samplers</div>
